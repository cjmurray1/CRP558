Exercise 12

Alterations to Mapbox Original Style

For my custom tile layer, I chose the 'Outdoors' tile as my template. I then changed the colors of water, green space, and labels. I also changed the visibility of road sign shield (like interstate numbers). In addition, I removed the icons of points of interest, but kept the text. One of the defining parts of the Outdoors template is the inclusion of topographical lines, so I change the units from meters to feet to help comprehension by American viewers. I added a KML file of the 2014 list of Impaired Streams of Iowa to my custom layer. I styled the lines to have a complementary color to the "natural landforms" color scheme that I developed earlier.
