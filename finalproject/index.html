Saylorville Lake Hiking Trails

Saylorville Lake is well known for its recreational value in the Des Moines Area, where it sees over 2 million visitors annually. Saylorville has four campgrounds, a disc golf course, four boat ramps, the 26 mile long Neal Smith trail and numerous picnic shelters. In addition, Saylorville is home to several hiking trails.
